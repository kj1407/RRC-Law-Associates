Custom Drafting of General (GPA) and Special (SPA) Instruments

A poorly drafted or overly broad Power of Attorney is one of the leading causes of property fraud and unauthorized asset transfers in India. Our corporate and real estate divisions mitigate this risk by drafting tailored General Power of Attorney (GPA) or transaction-specific Special Power of Attorney (SPA) instruments. We build strict restrictive parameters directly into the deed—explicitly defining boundaries for sale, mortgage, or court representation. This ensures your designated agent operates within clear legal bounds, keeping your real estate portfolio in Thanjavur, Kumbakonam, or neighboring districts fully secure.

Navigating Embassy Attestation and Local Adjudication Workflows

For a Power of Attorney executed outside India to be legally valid for property registrations or court filings, it must undergo a strict validation process. After executing the deed abroad, it must be formally attested by the local Indian Embassy or Consulate (or Apostilled in Hague Convention nations). Once sent to India, the instrument must be presented for mandatory adjudication before the competent District Registrar within three months of arrival, as regulated by the Indian Stamp Act. Our team directly manages this entire domestic registration pipeline, handling stamp duty computations and registry interfaces flawlessly.

POA Verification Audits and Revocation Frameworks

If you suspect your local representative or caretaker is abusing an active Power of Attorney, or if the underlying transaction is complete, immediate cancellation is essential. Under Indian property jurisprudence, a mere oral or un-notified cancellation cannot invalidate a registered deed. We systematically process formal revocations of Power of Attorney, drafting explicit Cancellation Deeds, executing mandatory registrations, and issuing public notices across widely circulated regional newspapers to completely terminate your agent's legal authority and protect your assets from unauthorized exposure.
